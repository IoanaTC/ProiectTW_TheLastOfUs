"The Last of Us" is a 2013 action-adventure game developed by Naughty Dog, featuring Joel, a smuggler tasked with escorting a teenage girl, Ellie, across a post-apocalyptic United States. Players can either use firearms and improvised weapons, or a stealthier aproach to defend against cannibalistic creatures infected by a mutated fungus in the genus Cordyceps.
